### Research

My general research interests are broad and cover most applications of deep learning however, I am particularly interested in representation learning, explainable AI, and self-supervised learning, and working on research that can help people. My PhD research is at the cross section of Natural Language Processing and Semantic Web. Specifically, how we can use findings in one field to improve results of the other.

Currently, I am working on a method for creating subevent aware event representations given a target knowledge graph ontology.
